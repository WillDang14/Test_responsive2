01. Overview

Ho Chi Minh City is the largest city in Vietnam, with a population of 9 million in 2019

02. Economy

The city is currently undergoing a period of strong economic and population growth; as it is the economic and financial hub of Vietnam, it continues to attract migrants from other provinces

03. Visiting time

The best time to visit Ho Chi Minh City is during the dry season, from December to March, when the weather is the most pleasant. Unsurprisingly, these months are also the busiest time to travel in Ho Chi Minh City. Expect heavy rainfall during the monsoon season from May to October

04. Things to do

Ho Chi Minh City is one of Vietnam's best places to visit. From delicious food to beautiful French architecture to an up-and-coming craft beer scene, you'll find no lack of things to do

05. Getting around

Despite its notorious traffic, getting around Ho Chi Minh City is a fairly easy task. It becomes more straightforward once you’ve done a bit of research before making your way to this lively city

Enjoy your time
